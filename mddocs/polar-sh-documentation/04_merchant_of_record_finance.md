# Merchant of Record & Finance

Polar acts as your Merchant of Record (MoR), meaning we are the legal reseller of your goods.

## Global Tax Compliance
-   **Liability**: Polar handles VAT, GST, and Sales Tax liability globally.
-   **Calculation**: Real-time tax calculation at checkout.
-   **Remittance**: Polar files and pays taxes to the appropriate authorities worldwide.
-   **B2B**: Handles EU VAT reverse charge for business customers.

## Payouts
-   **Provider**: Stripe Connect Express.
-   **Countries**: Supported in 70+ countries (including where Stripe is invite-only, like India).
-   **Thresholds**: Minimum balance required (e.g., $10 USD, $13 EUR).
-   **Fees**: Pass-through fees from Stripe (e.g., $0.25 + 0.25% per payout).
-   **Invoices**: Polar generates reverse invoices for your accounting records.

## Account Balance
-   **Net Revenue**: Your balance reflects revenue minus Polar's fees and collected taxes.
-   **Held Reserves**: Polar may hold reserves or delay payouts for new accounts to mitigate fraud risk.

## Refunds & Chargebacks
-   **Refunds**: You can issue full or partial refunds. Fees are generally not refunded to you.
-   **Chargebacks**: $15 fee per dispute. Polar proactively monitors chargeback rates. High rates (>0.7%) can lead to account suspension.
-   **Fraud Prevention**: Automated risk scoring and 60-day refund window at Polar's discretion to prevent disputes.

## Analytics
-   **Sales**: Revenue, Orders, AOV, MRR, Active Subscriptions.
-   **Costs**: (Beta) Track infrastructure/LLM costs alongside revenue to calculate margins and LTV.
