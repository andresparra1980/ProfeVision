# Products and Pricing

Polar treats everything as a product, whether it's a one-time purchase or a recurring subscription.

## Product Types

1.  **One-time Purchases**: Sell digital products, courses, or licenses with instant delivery.
2.  **Subscriptions**: Recurring billing (Monthly/Yearly) with automatic renewals.

## Pricing Models

-   **Fixed Price**: Set a specific amount.
-   **Pay What You Want**: Allow customers to pay any amount above a minimum.
-   **Free**: Offer free products to generate leads.

## Seat-Based Pricing

Sell team products where a billing manager purchases seats and assigns them to members.
-   **Tiers**: Define volume-based pricing (e.g., $10/seat for 1-4 seats, $9/seat for 5+).
-   **Management**: Billing managers can assign, revoke, and manage seats via the Customer Portal.
-   **API**: Full API support for assigning and managing seats programmatically.

## Discounts

-   **Types**: Percentage off, Fixed amount off.
-   **Duration**: Once, Repeating (several months), or Forever.
-   **Redemption Limits**: Limit the total number of redemptions.
-   **Codes**: Optional custom codes for customers to enter at checkout.

## Trials

Offer free trials for subscription products.
-   **Configuration**: Set duration in days, weeks, months, or years.
-   **Behavior**: Payment info collected upfront; charged only after trial ends.
-   **Abuse Prevention**: Optional setting to block repeated trials based on email or payment method fingerprint.

## ParityDeals Integration

Polar integrates with ParityDeals to offer Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) discounts, automatically adjusting prices based on the customer's location.
