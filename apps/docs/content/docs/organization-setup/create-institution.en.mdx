## Create institution

An institution represents your school, university, or educational center.

### Access institutions

1. In the sidebar, click **Institutions**
2. You'll see your institutions list (or an empty state if it's your first)

![Institutions list](/images/organization-setup/institution-01-list.webp)

### Add new institution

1. Click the **New Institution** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Name**: Your institution's name
   - **Type**: University, Institute, or School
3. Click **Create**

![Institution form](/images/organization-setup/institution-02-create-form.webp)

### Search institutions

Use the search field to filter by name or type.


<Callout type="info">
You can create multiple institutions if you teach at different places.
</Callout>
