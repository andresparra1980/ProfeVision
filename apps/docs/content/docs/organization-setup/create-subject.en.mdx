## Create subject

A subject represents a course or class you teach.

### Access subjects

1. In the sidebar, click **Subjects**
2. You'll see your subjects displayed as cards

![Subjects list](/images/organization-setup/subject-01-list.webp)

### Add new subject

1. Click the **New Subject** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Name**: Subject name (minimum 2 characters)
   - **Description**: Optional description
   - **Institution**: Select an institution (optional)
3. Click **Create**

![Subject form](/images/organization-setup/subject-02-create-form.webp)

### Edit or delete

Each card has buttons to:
- **Edit**: Modify name, description, or institution
- **Delete**: Remove the subject and associated data


<Callout type="warn">
Deleting a subject also deletes its groups, exams, and results.
</Callout>
