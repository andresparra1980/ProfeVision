## Troubleshooting

Solutions for the most common scanning problems.

### QR not detected

**Symptom**: "No exam detected"

**Causes and solutions**:

| Cause | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| QR cut off in photo | Ensure entire QR is visible |
| QR damaged or stained | Use another sheet or clean the QR |
| Blurry image | Keep phone stable |
| Low light | Improve lighting |

![QR not detected](/images/grading/scan-error-01-qr.webp)

### Incorrect answers

**Symptom**: Detected answers don't match marked ones

**Solutions**:

1. Verify bubbles are fully filled
2. Use darker pencil
3. Improve lighting
4. Avoid marks outside bubbles

### Exam not found

**Symptom**: "This exam doesn't belong to you"

**Causes**:

- Exam was created by another teacher
- Sheet is from a different exam

### Limit reached

**Symptom**: Can't scan more sheets

**Solution**: Upgrade to Plus for unlimited scans or wait for cycle reset.

### Image too large

**Symptom**: Error saving due to image size

**Solution**: System compresses automatically, but if it persists:

1. Take photo at lower resolution
2. Get closer to the sheet

### Slow processing

**Symptom**: Scan takes more than 10 seconds

**Causes**:

- Slow internet connection
- High server demand

**Solution**: Wait and retry if it fails.

<Callout type="info">
If a problem persists, contact support with a screenshot of the error.
</Callout>
