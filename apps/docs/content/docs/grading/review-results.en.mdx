## Review results

After processing, verify that answers were detected correctly.

### Information shown

You'll see the scan data:

- **Student**: Name and identification
- **Exam**: Title and subject
- **Group**: Student's group
- **Grade**: Score obtained

![Scan information](/images/grading/scan-05-results.webp)

### Detected answers

Bubbles show detected answers:

- **Blue**: Answer marked by student
- **Green**: Correct answer
- **Red**: Incorrect answer


### Correct answers

If an answer was detected incorrectly:

1. Click on the correct bubble
2. The answer updates
3. The grade recalculates

<Callout type="info">
Manual corrections are rarely needed. If it happens frequently, check print quality and lighting conditions.
</Callout>

### Verify grade

The grade is calculated automatically:

```
Grade = (Correct answers / Total questions) × Maximum score
```
