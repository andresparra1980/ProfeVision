## Start scanning

Access the scanner from anywhere in the dashboard.

### Floating button

The scan button appears in the **bottom-right corner** of your screen.

![Floating button](/images/grading/start-01-fab.webp)

### Check limits

Before scanning, the system verifies your quota:

- You'll see how many scans you've used
- If you reach the limit, a warning appears
- Plus users have unlimited scans

![Usage limits](/images/grading/start-02-limits.webp)

### Instructions screen

When starting you'll see instructions with tips:

1. Make sure you have good lighting
2. Place the sheet on a flat surface
3. Capture the entire sheet in the frame
4. Avoid shadows and reflections

![Instructions](/images/grading/start-03-instructions.webp)

### Continue

Click **Continue** to open the camera.

<Callout type="info">
The scanner works best with the phone in landscape position.
</Callout>
