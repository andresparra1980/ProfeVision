## Camera Grading

ProfeVision uses OMR (Optical Mark Recognition) technology to automatically grade exams with your phone's camera.

### How it works

1. Students mark their answers on printed sheets
2. You take a photo of each sheet with your phone
3. The system detects the marked answers
4. The grade is calculated and saved automatically
