## Capture image

Capture answer sheets with your device's camera.

### Open camera

When clicking the capture area, your device's camera opens.

![Capture area](/images/grading/capture-01-area.webp)

### Capture options

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Flash | Turn flash on/off |
| B/W | Black and white mode (better contrast) |

### How to capture

1. Place the sheet on a flat surface
2. Make sure the entire sheet is visible
3. Verify the QR is completely visible
4. Take the photo

![Correct capture](/images/grading/capture-02-correct.webp)

### Preview

After capturing:

1. Check that the image is clear
2. If not good, click **Retake**
3. If good, click **Continue**

![Preview](/images/grading/capture-03-preview.webp)

### Tips

- Use good natural or artificial lighting
- Avoid shadows on the sheet
- Don't tilt the phone too much
- Keep the phone stable

<Callout type="warn">
If the image is blurry or the QR isn't readable, the scan will fail.
</Callout>
