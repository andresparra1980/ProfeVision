## Upgrade to Plus

Unlock all features without limits.

### From My Plan

1. Go to **My Plan** in the sidebar
2. Click **Upgrade to Plus**
3. You'll be redirected to the payment page

![Upgrade button](/images/subscription/upgrade-01-button.webp)

### Payment process

1. Enter your payment details
2. Confirm the purchase
3. Your account updates automatically

![Payment page](/images/subscription/upgrade-02-checkout.webp)

### Payment methods

We accept:
- Credit/debit cards
- PayPal

### Activation

After payment:
- Your plan changes to Plus immediately
- Limits are removed
- You receive a confirmation email

### Billing

- Automatic monthly charge
- You can cancel anytime
- Invoices available in portal

<Callout type="info">
Payment is processed securely by Polar.
</Callout>
