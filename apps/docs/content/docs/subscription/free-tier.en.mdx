## Free Plan

The free plan lets you try ProfeVision at no cost.

### Monthly limits

| Resource | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| AI generations | 1 per month |
| Exam scans | 50 per month |
| Students | 100 total |
| Groups | 5 total |

### Features included

- Create exams manually
- Generate PDF answer sheets
- Scan and grade with camera
- View results and statistics
- Export to Excel
- Email support

### Limit reset

AI generation and scan limits reset each month, 30 days after your registration.


### When to upgrade

Consider upgrading to Plus if:

- You need more scans
- You want to use AI frequently
- You have more than 100 students
- You need more than 5 groups

<Callout type="info">
You can use the free plan indefinitely within the limits.
</Callout>
