## Subscription

ProfeVision offers a free plan and a Plus plan with unlimited features.

### Plan comparison

| Feature | Free | Plus |
|---------|------|------|
| AI generations | 1/month | Unlimited |
| Scans | 50/month | Unlimited |
| Students | 100 | Unlimited |
| Groups | 5 | Unlimited |
| Support | Email | Priority |
