## Usage tracking

Monitor how much you've used of your monthly limits.

### View your usage

1. In the sidebar, click **My Plan**
2. You'll see your current plan and usage


### Usage indicators

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| Green bar | Low usage (&lt;50%) |
| Yellow bar | Medium usage (50-80%) |
| Red bar | High usage (>80%) |

### On the dashboard

On the main panel you'll also see:

- AI generations used
- Scans used
- Days until reset


### Limit alerts

When approaching the limit:

- Warning appears at 80%
- At 100%, feature is blocked
- You can upgrade to Plus or wait for reset

<Callout type="warn">
If you reach the scan limit, you won't be able to grade more exams until reset.
</Callout>
