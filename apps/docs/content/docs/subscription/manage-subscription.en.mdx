## Manage subscription

Manage your subscription from the customer portal.

### Access the portal

1. Go to **My Plan**
2. Click **Manage subscription**
3. Polar portal opens


### Available options

In the portal you can:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| View invoices | Payment history |
| Update payment | Change payment method |
| Cancel | Cancel subscription |

### Cancel subscription

1. In the portal, click **Cancel**
2. Confirm cancellation
3. You keep Plus until period end

### After canceling

- Plus access until end of paid period
- After, you return to free plan
- Your data is preserved
- You can reactivate anytime

<Callout type="info">
Canceling doesn't delete your data. Only free plan limits apply.
</Callout>
