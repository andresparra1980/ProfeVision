## AI Generation

Use artificial intelligence to generate individual questions.

### Access

1. In the sidebar, click **Exams**
2. Click **Create with AI**

### How it works

This method combines the manual form with per-question AI generation:

1. Fill in the exam's general information
2. On each question, click **Generate with AI**
3. AI generates a question based on context
4. Edit or accept the generated question

![Generate AI button](/images/exam-creation/ai-gen-01-button.webp)

### Using the generator

1. Click the **Generate with AI** button (sparkles icon)
2. Enter the question's topic or context
3. Click **Generate**
4. Review the generated question
5. Accept or generate another

![Generation modal](/images/exam-creation/ai-gen-02-modal.webp)

### Edit result

After generating, you can:

- Modify the question text
- Change the options
- Adjust the correct answer
- Regenerate if not satisfied

![Generated question](/images/exam-creation/ai-gen-03-result.webp)

<Callout type="info">
AI generation consumes credits from your plan. Check your usage in **My Plan**.
</Callout>
