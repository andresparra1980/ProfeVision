## AI Chat

Create exams conversationally with AI assistance.

### Access

1. In the sidebar, click **Exams**
2. Click **Create with AI**

![AI chat access](/images/exam-creation/chat-01-access.webp)

### How it works

1. Describe the exam you need
2. AI asks questions to better understand
3. Generates questions based on your conversation
4. Review and adjust the result

![Chat interface](/images/exam-creation/chat-02-interface.webp)

### Conversation example

> **You**: I need a math exam about fractions for 5th grade
>
> **AI**: Got it. How many questions do you need? What operations do you want to include?
>
> **You**: 10 questions, addition and subtraction of fractions
>
> **AI**: Perfect. Generating the exam...

### Available options

- **Language**: Change the conversation language
- **Clear chat**: Delete conversation and start over
- **Save draft**: Save progress to continue later

![Options](/images/exam-creation/chat-03-options.webp)

### Save draft

1. Click **Save draft**
2. Choose a name for the draft
3. Continue later from where you left off

<Callout type="info">
Drafts are saved to your account and you can access them from the exams list.
</Callout>
