## QR Codes

Each answer sheet has a unique QR code that identifies the student.

### Location

The QR appears in the **top-left corner** of each answer sheet.

![QR location](/images/printing/qr-01-location.webp)

### Information contained

The QR contains:

- Exam ID
- Student ID
- Group ID
- Verification code

<Callout type="info">
The QR doesn't contain personal student data, only internal identifiers.
</Callout>

### QR importance

The QR is **essential** for:

1. **Automatically identifying** the student when scanning
2. **Linking answers** to the correct exam
3. **Avoiding errors** from manual assignment

### Care

- Don't fold the sheet over the QR
- Don't scratch or stain the QR area
- Ensure the QR is visible when scanning

### If QR is not detected

If the system doesn't detect the QR when scanning:

1. Make sure the QR is complete in the image
2. Verify it's not damaged or stained
3. Try scanning again with better lighting
4. As a last resort, manually assign the student
