## Print guidelines

Follow these recommendations to ensure successful scanning.

### Recommended paper

| Characteristic | Recommendation |
|----------------|----------------|
| Size | Letter or A4 |
| Color | White |
| Weight | 75-90 g/m² (normal paper) |

### Print settings

1. **Scale**: 100% (don't fit to paper)
2. **Orientation**: Portrait
3. **Margins**: Use document margins
4. **Color**: Black and white is enough

![Settings](/images/printing/print-01-settings.webp)

### Verifications before printing

- [ ] QR is readable in preview
- [ ] Bubbles appear complete
- [ ] Corner markers are visible
- [ ] No cuts on edges

### What to avoid

| Problem | Consequence |
|---------|-------------|
| Scaling the document | Distorted QR and bubbles |
| Printing on colored paper | Harder detection |
| Low print quality | Blurry bubbles |
| Folding sheets | Damages QR and markers |

### For students

Tell your students to:

- Use **dark pencil** or black pen
- Fill bubbles **completely**
- Don't make marks outside bubbles
- Don't fold or damage the sheet

<Callout type="warn">
Poor print quality can cause scanning errors.
</Callout>
