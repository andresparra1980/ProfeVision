## Dashboard overview

The dashboard is your operations center in ProfeVision.

### Sidebar

The left sidebar contains the main navigation:

- **Dashboard**: Overview and statistics
- **Institutions**: Manage your educational institutions
- **Subjects**: Manage your subjects
- **Groups**: Organize your student groups
- **Exams**: Create and manage exams
- **Students**: Manage students
- **My Plan**: View your subscription and limits
- **Settings**: Account settings

![Sidebar](/images/getting-started/dashboard-02-sidebar.webp)

### Main statistics

At the top you'll see 4 cards with:

- Total institutions
- Total subjects
- Active groups (and archived)
- Total students

### Grading section

Shows your grading progress:

- Graded exams
- Time saved
- Efficiency

### Plan usage

Indicates your current consumption:

- AI generations used
- Exam scans used
- Days until cycle reset

### Recent exams

List of your last 5 exams with quick access to edit or view results.

![Full dashboard](/images/getting-started/dashboard-01-overview.webp)

### Scan button

The floating button in the bottom right corner allows you to quickly scan exams.

<Callout type="info">
You can collapse the sidebar by clicking the panel icon at the top.
</Callout>
