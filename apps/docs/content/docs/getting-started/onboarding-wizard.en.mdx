## Setup wizard

The wizard guides you through 6 steps to set up your account. It takes about 2 minutes.

### Step 1: Welcome

Introduction screen. Click **Start**.

![Welcome step](/images/getting-started/onboarding-01-welcome-step.webp)

### Step 2: Create institution

Enter your institution details:

- **Name**: Your school or institution name
- **Type**: School, university, institute, academy, or other

![Create institution](/images/getting-started/onboarding-02-institution-step.webp)

### Step 3: Create subject

Create your first subject:

- **Institution**: Auto-selected
- **Name**: Subject name
- **Description**: Optional

![Create subject](/images/getting-started/onboarding-03-subject-step.webp)

### Step 4: Create group

Create your first student group:

- **Subject**: Auto-selected
- **Name**: Group name (e.g., "3rd A")
- **Year**: School year (optional)
- **Period**: School period (optional)

![Create group](/images/getting-started/onboarding-04-group-step.webp)

### Step 5: Add students

Add students to your group:

- **Manual**: Enter name and ID one by one
- **Import**: Upload an Excel file

![Add students](/images/getting-started/onboarding-05-students-step.webp)

### Step 6: Complete

Setup complete! You'll see recommended next steps.

![Complete](/images/getting-started/onboarding-06-complete-step.webp)

<Callout type="info">
Your progress is saved automatically. If you leave, you'll continue where you left off.
</Callout>
