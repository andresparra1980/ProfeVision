## Create an account

To use ProfeVision you need to create a free account.

### Steps

1. Go to the registration page
2. Fill in the form:
   - **First Name**: Your first name (min 2 characters)
   - **Last Name**: Your last name (min 2 characters)
   - **Email**: Your email address
   - **Password**: Minimum 6 characters
   - **Confirm Password**: Repeat your password
3. Complete the security CAPTCHA
4. Click **Create Account**

![Registration form](/images/getting-started/account-creation-01-register-form.webp)

### Verify your email

After registering:

1. Check your inbox
2. Find the email from ProfeVision
3. Click the verification link

<Callout type="info">
If you can't find the email, check your spam folder.
</Callout>

![Verification page](/images/getting-started/account-creation-02-verify-email.webp)

### Confirmation

After clicking the link you'll see a confirmation page. Your account is ready.

![Email confirmed](/images/getting-started/account-creation-03-email-confirmed.webp)
