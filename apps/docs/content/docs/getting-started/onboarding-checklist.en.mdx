## Initial task list

After the setup wizard, a floating list appears that guides you to complete your first exam.

### Location

- **Desktop**: Right sidebar (expandable/collapsible)
- **Mobile**: Bottom bar (above the scan button)

![Floating checklist](/images/getting-started/checklist-01-floating.webp)

### The 4 tasks

Complete these tasks in order:

| # | Task | Action |
|---|------|--------|
| 1 | **Create exam** | Import a document or use AI chat |
| 2 | **Publish exam** | Change status from draft to published |
| 3 | **Export PDF** | Download answer sheets |
| 4 | **First scan** | Scan a sheet with the camera |

![Checklist tasks](/images/getting-started/checklist-02-tasks.webp)

### Progress

- Progress bar shows your advancement
- Completed tasks appear crossed out with ✓
- You can only do the next task in order

### Create your first exam

When clicking "Create exam":

1. A panel opens with options
2. **Import PDF/Word**: Upload an existing exam
3. **Create with AI**: Chat with AI to create it

![Creation options](/images/getting-started/checklist-03-create-drawer.webp)

### Hide the checklist

- Click the **X** to minimize
- Reappears when changing pages until first 3 tasks complete
- Disappears permanently when all tasks are done
