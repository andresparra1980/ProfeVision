## Account issues

### I can't log in

**Possible causes:**
- Wrong password
- Email not verified
- Account doesn't exist

**Solutions:**
1. Verify the email is correct
2. Use "Forgot password" to reset
3. Check spam folder for verification email

### I'm not receiving the verification email

1. Check your spam/promotions folder
2. Wait a few minutes
3. Request resend from login page
4. Verify email address is correct

### Can I change my email?

Not directly. Contact support to request a change.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to the data deletion page
2. Follow the instructions
3. Your account and data will be deleted

<Callout type="warn">
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Callout>

### My session keeps logging out

- Clear browser cookies
- Verify you're not using incognito mode
- Try a different browser
