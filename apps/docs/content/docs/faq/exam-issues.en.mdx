## Exam issues

### I can't create an exam

**Prerequisites:**
1. Have at least one institution
2. Have at least one subject
3. Have at least one group

Create these elements first in **Organization Setup**.

### I can't edit a published exam

Published exams have limited editing to protect results. You can:
- Enable/disable questions
- Change the correct answer

### Can I duplicate an exam?

Currently there's no duplicate function. Create a new exam or use AI.

### How do I delete an exam?

1. Go to the exams list
2. Only **draft** exams can be deleted
3. Click delete and confirm

<Callout type="warn">
Published exams cannot be deleted if they have results.
</Callout>

### PDF won't generate

- Verify exam has questions
- Exam must be published
- Must have at least one assigned group

### Questions didn't save

Your progress saves in the browser. If you changed devices or cleared data, it was lost. Complete exam in one session.
