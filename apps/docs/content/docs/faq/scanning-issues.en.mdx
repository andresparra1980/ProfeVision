## Scanning issues

### QR is not detected

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure complete QR is in the photo
2. Improve lighting
3. Avoid shadows on the QR
4. Don't fold the sheet over the QR
5. Clean the camera

### Answers are detected wrong

**Possible causes:**
- Poorly filled bubbles
- Light pencil
- Blurry image

**Solutions:**
1. Tell students to fill completely
2. Use dark pencil or pen
3. Keep phone stable when capturing

### "No exam detected"

System couldn't read the QR. Check:
- QR visible and complete
- Correct sheet (from correct exam)
- Good lighting

### "This exam doesn't belong to you"

Sheet belongs to another teacher. Verify it's your exam.

### Scanning is very slow

- Check your internet connection
- Wait and retry
- Server may be busy

<Callout type="info">
If an error persists, capture a screenshot and contact support.
</Callout>
