## Billing issues

### Payment was declined

**Possible causes:**
- Insufficient funds
- Expired card
- Card limit reached
- Bank blocked transaction

**Solutions:**
1. Verify card details
2. Contact your bank
3. Try a different payment method

### Where do I see my invoices?

1. Go to **My Plan**
2. Click **Manage subscription**
3. In Polar portal, go to "Invoices"

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to **My Plan**
2. Click **Manage subscription**
3. Select "Cancel"
4. Keep Plus until period end

### Are there refunds?

Contact support within the first 7 days of your subscription to request a refund.

### Payment processed but I don't have Plus

Wait a few minutes. If it persists:
1. Log out and log back in
2. Contact support with payment receipt

<Callout type="info">
Payments are processed securely by Polar.
