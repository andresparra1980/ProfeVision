## Profile

Update your personal information.

### Editable fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| First name | Your first name |
| Last name | Your last name |
| Email | Your email (read-only) |

### Edit profile

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Modify the fields
3. Click **Save**


### Email

Your email cannot be changed directly. If you need to change it, contact support.

<Callout type="info">
Your name appears on answer sheets and reports.
</Callout>
